WHAT'S THE WATER MEANS TO YOU?

PERMANENCE?

TRANSIENCE

Lao Tzu wrote:

"Highest good is like water. Because water excels in benefiting the myriad creatures without contending with them and settles where none would like to be, it comes close to the Way."

In a culture that often values aggressive striving and rigid control, the Daoist philosophy of water reminds us that true resilience comes from flexibility, not stubborn hardness.
